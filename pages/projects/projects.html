Shootout Contest

Live Demo
Code

Click on the Start button, and after short music intro, targets will appear randomly on their spots. Earned points depends on your precision. 'Green' circle is 'Bullseye', and it brings 12 points. As you progress, targets will appear faster. Every time you miss, you lose one life. Number of lives is represented by number of shiny bullets left.

Weather App

Live Demo
Code

Weather app is responsive, and works behind the scene with two third party API-s. First is Reverse Geocoding and Geolocation Service API, and second is Open Weather Map API. On load event, app retrieves and stores browsers geolocation information(lat, lon) from Navigator.Geolocation Web API. That data is used for reversed geocoding, if user choice is to see weather options for the location of his presence...
